===== ppt/slides/slide9.xml =====
ANSWER ENGINE OPTIMIZATION
Be the direct answer
AEO
Optimise for featured snippets, voice search and AI answer boxes — win “position zero”, not just a link.
What to do
Answer the question in the first 40–60 words of the page
Target long-tail, question-form keywords (how / what / cost)
Add a dedicated FAQ block to every service &amp; blog page
Use concise, scannable paragraphs and clear sub-headings
Provide definitions, steps and comparison tables AI can lift
How we ship it
FAQPage + HowTo JSON-LD on relevant templates
A reusable “Quick answer” box at the top of each page
Question-based H2/H3 headings mapped to real queries
Speakable schema for voice-assistant readiness
Internal search-intent map kept in the CMS
===== ppt/slides/slide10.xml =====
GENERATIVE ENGINE OPTIMIZATION
Get summarised &amp; cited
GEO
Earn citations inside Google AI Overviews, Perplexity and SearchGPT by being the clearest, most trustworthy source.
What to do
Build deep topical authority around each service silo
Lead with original data, examples, stats and case studies
Keep entity names (brand, services, locations) consistent
Demonstrate E-E-A-T: authors, credentials, citations
Target niche, India-specific intents AI training data lacks
How we ship it
Topic clusters: pillar pages + supporting articles, interlinked
Author bios with Person schema + sameAs profile links
Key-takeaways summary box on long content
Fresh “last updated” dates and a real publishing cadence
Monitor how ChatGPT / Gemini / Perplexity describe the brand
===== ppt/slides/slide11.xml =====
LARGE LANGUAGE MODEL OPTIMIZATION
Speak the machines’ language
LLMO
Structure and tag content so ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini can parse, trust and reference it accurately.
What to do
Use a clean, logical H2/H3 structure on every page
Write self-contained sections that make sense out of context
State facts plainly — brand, services, pricing, location
Maintain consistent business info across the whole web
Aim for contextual completeness, not keyword stuffing
How we ship it
JSON-LD: Organization, Service, Article, Person, Breadcrumb
Add an llms.txt file pointing AI bots to your best content
Allow GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended
Server-side render so content is visible without JavaScript
Keep Google Business Profile + directories in sync
