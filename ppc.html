QUICK ANSWER · AEOWhat is PPC and how does it work?

PPC (pay-per-click) is a paid advertising model where you pay each time someone clicks your ad on platforms like Google, Meta or LinkedIn. Done well, it delivers immediate, measurable traffic and leads — with budget, targeting and creative continuously optimised toward the lowest cost per acquisition.

Paid traffic that pays for itself

PPC is the fastest way to put your offer in front of buyers — but it punishes guesswork. Wasted spend hides in broad targeting, weak landing pages and untracked conversions.

We build campaigns around tight intent, rigorous conversion tracking and constant optimisation, so your budget flows to what actually drives leads and sales.
