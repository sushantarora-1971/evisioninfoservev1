QUICK ANSWER · AEOWhat is mobile app marketing?

Mobile app marketing helps increase an app's visibility, downloads, engagement and retention — combining App Store Optimization (ASO), conversion-rate optimisation and growth campaigns across app stores and beyond.

More downloads, better retention

Getting an app built is only half the battle — being found and kept is the rest. Most app growth hides in store visibility and the conversion of a listing into an install.

We optimise your app store presence (ASO), improve install conversion (App CRO), and run growth campaigns that acquire the right users and keep them engaged.
