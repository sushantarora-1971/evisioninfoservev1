Technical SEO makes a site easy for search engines to crawl, render and index — covering site speed, Core Web Vitals, structured data, indexation and architecture, so your content can actually rank.

Fix the foundations rankings depend on

Great content can't rank on a broken foundation. We find and fix the crawl, indexation, speed and structured-data issues holding your site back.

Every fix is prioritised by impact and shipped with your dev team, then verified — no guesswork.
