QUICK ANSWER · AEOWhat is content marketing?

Content marketing is the practice of creating and distributing valuable, relevant content — articles, guides, videos and more — to attract and retain a clearly defined audience. For search, it builds the topical authority and answer-ready material that earns rankings on Google and citations inside AI engines.

Authority is built, one topic at a time

AI engines and Google both favour sources that demonstrably own a topic. That authority doesn't come from one viral post — it comes from deep, interlinked coverage that answers real questions completely.

We plan content in clusters, write it answer-first with proper schema, and interlink it so every piece lifts the others. The result is content that ranks, gets cited, and keeps working for years.
