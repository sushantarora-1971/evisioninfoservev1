QUICK ANSWER · AEOWhat is SEO in 2026?

SEO in 2026 is the practice of optimising a website to rank on Google and to be understood and cited by AI engines like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity. It spans technical health, on-page content, local signals and structured data — all working together to maximise visibility.

One discipline, four search layers

Most agencies still treat SEO as a checklist for ten blue links. We treat it as the system that makes your brand discoverable everywhere people now search — classic results, featured snippets, voice answers and AI summaries alike.

That means technical foundations, genuinely useful on-page content, strong local signals and machine-readable structure, engineered together. Each reinforces the others, and ~80% of the work compounds across every layer.
