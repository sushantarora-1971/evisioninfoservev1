QUICK ANSWER · AEOWhat is technical SEO?

Technical SEO is the practice of optimising a website's infrastructure — crawlability, indexation, site speed, structured data and mobile-readiness — so search engines and AI answer engines can efficiently discover, understand and rank its content. It's the foundation every other SEO effort depends on.

Your rankings are only as strong as your technicals

Without clean technical foundations, even the best content stays invisible. Search crawlers waste budget on broken URLs, slow pages get demoted, and AI engines have nothing reliable to ingest. We fix the plumbing first — then everything you publish performs better.

Every site we touch is audited against a 40-point technical checklist and rebuilt to meet Core Web Vitals, schema and indexation standards that hold up across Google, Bing and the new generation of generative search.

What's included

Crawlability & indexation

Robots.txt, XML sitemaps, canonicals and crawl-budget optimisation.

Core Web Vitals

LCP, INP & CLS tuning for ranking and a faster experience.

Structured data (JSON-LD)

Organization, Service, FAQ, Article, Breadcrumb & Product schema.

Mobile-first & HTTPS

Responsive layouts, secure delivery and clean, descriptive URLs.

AI crawler access

llms.txt plus access rules for GPTBot, ClaudeBot & PerplexityBot.

Internal link architecture

Logical silos and 3–5 contextual links per page to spread authority.
