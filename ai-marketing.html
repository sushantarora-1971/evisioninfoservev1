QUICK ANSWER · AEOWhat is AI digital marketing?

AI digital marketing uses artificial intelligence both as a channel and a tool — optimising content to be cited by generative engines (GEO/LLMO), and using AI to automate campaigns, personalise outreach and analyse performance at scale. It's how modern brands stay visible as search shifts from links to answers.

Win the search layer that didn't exist three years ago

As more searches end inside AI answers, being the source those answers cite becomes as valuable as ranking #1. That requires content structured for machines and a way to measure whether it's working.

We optimise your content for generative engines, open the right doors for AI crawlers, and put automation and monitoring in place — so you both earn AI citations and operate more efficiently.
